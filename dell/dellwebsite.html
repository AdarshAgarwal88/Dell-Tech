Overview

As Indian industry gears up to seize the opportunities of
the new year – much of them enabled by the power of
technology, CIOs and IT leaders are mandated today to
solve complex problems with technology made for
innovation. Across sectors, it is amply clear that endpoint
users are demanding more from their machines; not least
of all – the ability to extract valuable insights from vast
amounts of data.

As Digital Transformation initiatives and the race to hyper-
competitiveness accelerates, industry is demanding
workstations that deliver the power to deploy and manage
cognitive technology platforms, including Machine
Learning (ML), Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Deep Learning
(DL).

Across sectors, the ability for endpoints to cater to a wide
spectrum of complex issues – ranging from healthcare,
energy, finance and manufacturing – the future will belong
to cognitive endpoints that promise everything from
solving to predictive insights!

hear eminent business, IT and technology leaders in
conversation.
